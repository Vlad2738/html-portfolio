The best movies according to Vlad

My top movies of all time.

Lord of the Rings

An extraordinary adventure that combines incredible real locations with great-looking models, visual effects, excellent performances and memorable characterizations, managing to immerse the viewer in Middle Earth.

Harry Potter

A red-blooded adventure movie, dripping with atmosphere, filled with the gruesome and the sublime, and surprisingly faithful to the novel. It's still a very entertaining and spectacular movie, with a rush of nostalgia to go alongside the exhilaration of fun.

Maze Runner

With strong acting, a solid premise, and a refreshingly dark approach to its dystopian setting, The Maze Runner stands out from the crowded field of YA sci-fi adventures.
